Understanding Corrugation Strength & Selection
Corrugation isn't one-size-fits-all. Single-wall corrugation provides economical protection for lightweight items and internal applications. Double-wall offers the sweet spot for most ecommerce and shipping applications, providing superior protection without excessive weight that increases shipping costs. Triple-wall corrugation handles the most demanding applications—heavy products, long-distance shipping, and export markets requiring maximum security. Our engineering team analyzes your specific product weight, shape, and shipping routes to recommend optimal corrugation levels. This consultation prevents costly over-specification while ensuring adequate protection—two factors that directly impact your bottom line.
