Return & Refund Policy

At Brands Face Packaging, we stand behind the quality of our products. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures.

1. Bespoke & Custom Orders

Since a majority of our packaging solutions are custom-manufactured to your specific dimensions, design, and branding requirements, we have special considerations:

  • No returns after production has commenced: Once manufacturing begins, we cannot accept returns or provide refunds.
  • Quality guarantee: If you receive the order and there are quality issues or manufacturing defects, we will address them immediately with reprints or corrections at no additional cost.
  • Refunds before production: If you request a refund before production has started, we can process the refund in full.
Important: Please review your design proof and specifications carefully before approving production. Once production starts, changes are not possible.

2. Standard Products

For non-custom, standard products, we offer the following return policy:

  • Return period: 14 days from the date of delivery
  • Condition: Products must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging
  • Return shipping: Return shipping costs may apply depending on the order size
  • Inspection: All returned items will be inspected to ensure they meet return criteria

3. Refund Processing

Once we receive and verify your return:

  • Refunds are processed within 7 business days after return approval
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Please allow 5-10 additional business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • For international returns, refund times may vary based on banking processes

4. Quality Issues & Defects

If you receive a product with manufacturing defects or quality issues:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos/evidence of the defect
  • We will provide a replacement or remanufactured product at no cost
  • Return shipping for defective items is covered by Brands Face Packaging
  • For custom orders, we'll reprint with corrections at no additional charge

5. Design Approval & Approval Proofs

Your responsibility includes:

  • Reviewing design proofs carefully before approving production
  • Verifying all specifications, dimensions, colors, and branding elements
  • Providing clear feedback on revision requests before approval
  • Understanding that approved designs cannot be changed during production

6. What Cannot Be Returned

  • Items that have been used or damaged by the customer
  • Custom orders after production has started
  • Items purchased on final sale or clearance
  • Damaged goods caused by improper storage or handling

7. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  • Contact us at hello@brandsface.com with your order number and reason for return
  • Provide photos of the product and packaging if claiming a defect
  • Obtain a return authorization number from our team
  • Ship the item back to us with the authorization number clearly marked
  • We will verify and process your refund once received and inspected

8. Exchanges

If you need a different size, design, or specification:

  • Exchanges for custom orders may be possible before production starts
  • After production has commenced, additional charges for changes may apply
  • Contact us immediately to discuss exchange options

9. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Custom design files and artwork
  • Tooling and setup charges
  • Rush production fees
  • Samples and prototypes

10. Disputes & Escalation

If you have a dispute regarding a return or refund:

  • Contact our customer service team with detailed information
  • We will work with you to find a fair resolution
  • Our team will respond within 48 hours of your inquiry
