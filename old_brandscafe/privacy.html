Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Brands Face Packaging, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, safeguard, and share your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Company details and business information
  • Project specifications and design files
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Communicating with you about your packaging needs
  • Sending quotes and project updates
  • Improving our services and website
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer support
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Use of secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Encryption of sensitive information including file uploads
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal data within our organization
  • Secure storage of all project files and communications

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your data is kept confidential and used solely for:

  • Order processing and fulfillment
  • Service provider partnerships (only when necessary)
  • Legal compliance and law enforcement requests
  • Protecting our rights and preventing fraud

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information (in certain circumstances)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site and to improve functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page or by sending you a notice.
